FLASHBACK MUSIC

This is the first time I learned what stand up meetings were. I worked alongside a group of 6 other individuals to create a music app that had certain requirements laid out by our professor. I again worked with Android Studio, but this time it was more hands on than before. Because of the background that I have, I was assigned to be the app designer with another student and mainly focused on making the app look good and make it more user friendly. The app was supposed to play songs you had previously played in that same location at around the same time. For example, if you walked your dog at 6pm on a Wednesday and heard the song "We Will Rock You" by Queen, next Wednesday at around 6 it would play that same song. It does it based off of time, day of the week, and location.
